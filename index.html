The Movement

We Are The
Wrong Crowd

As misunderstanding sparked a reaction in LOWERCASE, he began to notice the juxtaposition between raccoons and the members of his community. Raccoons are intelligent mammals like us who have been ostracized by a society that had labelled them as rodents. This misconception maximized the survival instinct of raccoons, forcing them to eventually fall into the typecasting they had been placed inside.

LOWERCASE created the concept of WRNG CRWD to symbolize and serve as a beacon for those who do not fit into the caricature of how black people are perceived. Blending the two experiences together, LOWERCASE and SELF along with the WRNG CRWD community have been able to showcase themselves as well as other talented black artists in the city through the 'Month's End' shows they have curated.

These shows served as a hub where attendees could network and share their talents with other like-minded black artists, photographers, cinematographers, designers, actors and actresses.

LOWERCASE

Artist • Producer • Founder

Canadian-American artist and producer LOWERCASE is establishing himself as an important talent and community fixture with no sights of slowing down. Growing up with frequent trips to learn his roots in Memphis and Jamaica, LOWERCASE was shaped to develop and nurture an eclectic ear for different sounds, which led him to explore creating his own distinct style. Hailing from Scarborough, Canada, his early influences stem from Jay-Z, Memphis artists Gangsta Blacc, Three 6 Mafia, 8 Ball & MJG, as well as Jamaican artists Bob Marley and Beres Hammond. From dissecting The Black Album on his mother's iPod nano to developing various projects, LOWERCASE continues to expand and magnetize his audience.

SELF

Artist • Producer • Wrong Crowd

SELF is an urban artist from Toronto, Canada, and a key member of the collective Wrong Crowd. As a conduit for the emotions and experiences of others, he channels the tribulations of life through his music, blending influences of hip-hop, R&B, jazz, neo-soul, lo-fi, soft rock, and house music. With a versatile mix of styles and cadences, SELF expresses his perspectives through soothing, somber tones, evoking deep emotion through both composition and lyrical selection. With a primary focus on sample-based production, SELF has cultivated a distinctive sound that resonates with listeners and is easily identifiable as his own.

BE

Vocalist • Collaborator • Wrong Crowd

Featured vocalist and collaborator within the Wrong Crowd collective, Be brings a refreshing and vulnerable perspective to the group's musical projects. Her presence on SELF's "Nights" from the ROADS EP introduced a dimension of intimacy and emotional depth, balancing energy with reflection. Be's vocal style carries a warmth and honesty that complements the collective's genre-blending approach across hip-hop, R&B, and neo-soul.

The Community

WRNG CRWD EXTENDED

JOSHUA OBRA
Actor • Writer • Break2Legs

Born and raised in Scarborough, Ontario, Joshua is an actor and writer with a passion for storytelling. His breakthrough role in Scarborough (2021) premiered at TIFF, and he returned to the festival with his lead performance in Express (2024). He's also the co-founder of Break2Legs, a platform giving back to up-and-coming actors through resources and educational programming.
DASHAWN BLACKWOOD
Actor • Content Creator

Deshean's work in acting and content creation expands the WRNG CRWD universe beyond music, showcasing the multifaceted talent that defines the collective. A key creative voice within the community.
MATTHEW LEE
Woodworker • Craftsman • Designer

Matthew Wai-Yiu Lee is a custom woodworker based in Toronto, designing and building bespoke cabinetry and furniture from idea to installation. From built-in desks and coffee tables to front doors, his craftsmanship brings the WRNG CRWD ethos into the physical world.
