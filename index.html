// The Collective
"As misunderstanding sparked a reaction, LOWERCASE began to notice the juxtaposition between raccoons and the members of his community."

Raccoons are intelligent mammals who have been ostracized by a society that labelled them as rodents. This misconception maximized their survival instinct, forcing them to fall into the typecasting they'd been placed inside. WRNG CRWD was created to symbolize and serve as a beacon for those who do not fit into the caricature of how Black people are perceived.

Blending both experiences, LOWERCASE and SELF—along with the WRNG CRWD community—have showcased themselves and other talented Black artists through the Month's End shows they curate: a hub where attendees network and share their talents across music, photography, film, design, and acting.

// Live
Month's End

Month's End is a recurring event series curated by WRNG CRWD—a space where Black artists, photographers, cinematographers, designers, actors, and musicians come together to network, perform, and build community.

Each show is more than a concert. It's an ecosystem: a hub for like-minded creatives who have been told they don't belong, proving that the wrong crowd was right all along.
